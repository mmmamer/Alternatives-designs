SRC

Self Balancing Generator

Our in house designed and devloped SRC Self Balancing Generator.

The Project continues. The Mk1 24/40 Unit has just completed a 28 day test run from the 11th March 2015 to the 7th April 2015. The unit ran 672 hours under 3/4 load.

During October 2015 The Improved Mk1 24/40 Unit completed two 10 day test runs under 3/4 load. One without the new Combined Fan Flywheel. One with the new Combined Fan Flywheel. The Combined Fan Flywheel proved a great success and is to be incorporated in all new versions.
